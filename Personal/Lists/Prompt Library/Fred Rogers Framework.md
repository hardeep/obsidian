**Emotional Safety → Meaning → Agency**.

### 1. Emotional Safety (Attachment Layer)

### 2. Meaning (Interpretive Layer)

- Describe how children at this age commonly interpret or misunderstand this topic.

---

### 3. Agency (Moral Autonomy Layer)

- Show how to give the child choice, voice, and participation in thinking about this topic.
    
- Provide reflective questions instead of commands.
    
- Explain how the child can act, decide, or feel empowered in relation to the topic.
    

---

### Guardrails (Do Not Do)

- List common adult responses that undermine emotional safety, distort meaning, or remove agency.
    
- Explicitly avoid fear-based compliance, shame, supernatural surveillance, or conditional love.
